Favorite Albums & Reviews

Parachutes
What I really liked about this album were songs like "Yellow" and "Sparks", which were some of the most soothing songs to vibe out to.
A Head Full of Dreams
This album contained various hits, but my favorite has to be "Adventure of a Lifetime" soley because of how well the vocals and instruments blended together, making it something much more upbeat and pleasant to listen to.
Ghost Stories
In all honesty, the songs from this album are pretty good, but the only song I keep downloaded is "A Sky Full of Stars ". Its mix of upbeat tempo and slow pace are what make me play this song on repeat from time to time.
